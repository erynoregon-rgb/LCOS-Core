# Public Disclosure Notice

This repository is a public toy package.

It is intended to demonstrate engineering discipline, testing rigor, and
claim-safe release practices. It is not intended to disclose non-public system
details, production configuration, or private implementation advantage.

## Important limitations

- The ledger is append-oriented by interface and tamper-aware by hash-chain
  verification.
- The ledger is not an OS-enforced immutable log.
- The ledger is not a signed or adversary-proof transparency system.
- The demos use synthetic inputs only.
- Research notes are concept-level discussion, not production evidence.
